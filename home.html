Aim of the college


1.To impart fundamental knowledge in the humanities, social sciences, science
and law in order to develop a committed and dedicated young
talent to serve the nation.


2.To inculcate the values of humanism, secularism, democracy, scientific temper
and intellectual capacity in young students to face the challenges
of changing society.


3.To ensure free access and equal opportunity of higher education to all students irrespective of caste, creed, race, region, religion and socio
economic status.
